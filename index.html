An open community meetup to hack on science projects every Thursday

What is this meetup?

Just a small gathering of scientists, researchers, and technologists working on neat projects. Bring your projects, get help and support, share new ideas, and meet people.

What kind of science hacking is allowed?

Anything that mixes science with code! Or doesn't. That's cool too.

Code of conduct

Our meetup is dedicated to providing a harassment-free conference experience for everyone, regardless of gender, gender identity and expression, age, sexual orientation, disability, physical appearance, body size, race, ethnicity, religion (or lack thereof), or technology choices. We do not tolerate harassment of participants in any form.
